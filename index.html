about us

The Alpine Hopper studio

Pikasagi builds flat, playful software—pastel, calm, and a little whimsical. Think matte cards, gentle color, and tools that feel friendly the first time you open them.

We think of our work as app building: careful and hands-on, one release at a time.

The Alpine Hopper is our mascot spirit: part alpine pika, part うさぎ, all bounce. We make things for players and anyone who wants a gentler, cuter interface to real life.
